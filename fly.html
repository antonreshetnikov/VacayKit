Known as one of the most popular holiday destinations on earth, Thailand has so much to offer travellers that it’s hard to know where to begin. From the golden sandy beaches to the rolling mountains of the North, antique shopping and dining like a king - Thailand has it all.

The south of Thailand has some of the most beautiful beaches and resorts in the world, and going underwater you can find colourful reefs and exotic sea creatures. Bangkok is an Asian metropolis like no other, packed with shopping and fine dining at a fraction of western prices, while the North of Thailand offers culture, a chance to visit remote hill tribes, elephant sanctuaries and treks to hidden waterfalls.
